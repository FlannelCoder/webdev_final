Background

WeatherNova was launched in January 2023, with the primary focus at the time being to provide no-hype, accurate, and reliable information on active tropical cyclones worldwide. Since then, over 200 videos have been uploaded in 2023 and 2024, covering most major tropical cyclones in each year.

Looking Forward: 2025

As 2024 comes to a close and 2025 begins, the aim of WeatherNova remains unchanged. WeatherNova will continue to provide the same videos it has over the past 2 years, alongside some venturing into new projects.

In mid-2023, a new aspect of WeatherNova begun, creating easy-to-understand plots for historical analysis, realtime analysis, and forecasts. This part is continuing to grow, and is now being brought public in the form of a Discord bot. The aim of the Discord bot is to give users greater access to the plots created by WeatherNova. More plots will be created, and if you have any feedback or suggestions for plots seen on this site or to be made, please leave them in the form below.

Looking long-term, the goal is to be able to bring created plots to a wider audience via the web, alongside creating a full website to host a WeatherNova blog, alongside the usual videos of course.
